Accessibility

Making The Reiki Space welcoming and comfortable for everyone.

Everyone is welcome at The Reiki Space. If there is anything that would make your visit or your session easier or more comfortable, please tell me before you book and I will do my best to accommodate you.

Our Commitment

I want every client to feel safe, respected, and at ease — regardless of age, ability, background, or health. Reiki is a gentle, non-invasive practice, and sessions can be adapted in many ways to suit your individual needs.

Adapting Your Session

Sessions are flexible and can be tailored to you. For example:

  • Reiki can be given hands-on or entirely hands-off, according to your comfort.
  • You remain fully clothed throughout, and can lie down or remain seated.
  • Lighting, music, and room temperature can be adjusted to suit sensory preferences.
  • If you have mobility needs, chronic pain, or find lying flat difficult, let me know and we will find a comfortable position together.
  • You are welcome to bring a friend, carer, or companion to your appointment.

If you have specific requirements, please mention them when booking so I can prepare in advance.

Visiting the Treatment Space

If you would like to know about access to the treatment space in Buxton before booking — including parking, steps, or any other practical considerations — please get in touch and I will happily talk you through it.

Distance Sessions

If travelling to Buxton is difficult for any reason, distance Reiki sessions are available from the comfort of your own home, conducted via WhatsApp (video or voice call), Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, or phone. Many clients find these just as supportive as in-person sessions.

Website Accessibility

This website has been built with accessibility in mind, including readable fonts, clear colour contrast, text that scales when you zoom, descriptive image text, and keyboard-friendly navigation. I am continually working to improve the experience and welcome any feedback.

Tell me what you need

If you have an access requirement, or if you experience any difficulty using this website, please contact Thomas at thereikispacebuxton@gmail.com or 07588 295 780. Your feedback helps me make The Reiki Space better for everyone.
