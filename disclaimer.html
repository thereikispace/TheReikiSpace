Nature of Reiki

Reiki is a holistic complementary practice intended to support relaxation and general well-being. It is not a medical treatment and makes no claim to diagnose, prevent, treat, or cure any physical or mental health condition.

Individual responses to Reiki vary. Any experiences, sensations, or outcomes described by previous clients reflect their personal experiences and are not a guarantee of results for any other individual.

Medical Conditions

If you have a diagnosed medical condition, are pregnant, or are currently receiving medical treatment, please consult your GP or specialist before booking a Reiki session. Reiki is safe and non-invasive, but we want to ensure every session is appropriate and comfortable for you.

Please inform Thomas of any health conditions, medications, or concerns prior to your session so that appropriate adjustments can be made if necessary.

Mental Health

Reiki can be a supportive complement to mental health care. However, it is not a replacement for professional mental health treatment, therapy, or counselling. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact your GP, a mental health professional, or call the Samaritans on 116 123.
