Distance Reiki Sessions, Available UK-Wide & Worldwide

Remote Reiki you can receive from the comfort of your own home.

Distance Reiki allows you to receive a full Reiki treatment without leaving home. Whether you are elsewhere in the UK, living overseas, recovering from illness, or simply prefer the comfort of your own surroundings, we meet online at an agreed time and share a session wherever you are.

Many people choose distance Reiki because it fits easily around work, family life or limited mobility, while others simply appreciate being able to relax in familiar surroundings.

What is distance Reiki?

Distance Reiki, sometimes called remote Reiki or absent healing, is a Reiki session where practitioner and client are not in the same room. Within the Reiki tradition, energy is not considered bound by physical location, so a session given remotely is understood to work in the same way as one given hands-on or hands-off in person. Many clients are surprised by how present and settled a remote session feels, and often find it difficult to tell the difference from being in the treatment room.

During your session

Ahead of our session, I begin by spending time connecting with Universal Life Force Energy through a practice known in the Reiki tradition as gassho. During the session I act as a conduit for Reiki, allowing it to flow to where it is needed. I work intuitively, often with an awareness of the chakra system and subtle energy field, and I tailor the session to you as an individual.

How we connect

Before your appointment, you choose the platform you are most comfortable with. The options are as follows:

WhatsApp

Video or voice call using your smartphone.

Google Meet

A simple video link that works on phones, tablets and computers.

Microsoft Teams

Ideal if you’re already familiar with Teams.

Telephone

Perfect if you’d rather keep things simple and avoid video altogether.

If technology feels daunting, don’t worry. A straightforward phone call is absolutely sufficient, and someone close to you is welcome to help get everything set up before leaving you to enjoy the session.

For clients outside the UK, appointments can be arranged across different time zones.

Is distance Reiki right for me?

Distance Reiki can be a practical option for almost anyone. Clients come to distance Reiki for many reasons, commonly to unwind from ongoing stress, to ease anxiety, or to support more restful sleep.

A remote session can be particularly welcome for anyone who is housebound or finds travel difficult. If someone you care about would like to experience Reiki but cannot easily get to a treatment room, a session can come to them instead.
